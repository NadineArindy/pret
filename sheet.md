  1. Controls
   * W, A, S, D: Move the active chef.
   * E: Interact (Pick up, Place down, Chop, Cook, Serve). This is your main action button.
   * TAB: Switch control between the two chefs.

  ---

  2. Map Legend (Where do I go?)
  The map uses specific letters to tell you what each station is.

  Ingredients (Left Side)
   * K: Rice (Kome)
   * N: Nori (Seaweed)
   * F: Fish
   * U: Cucumber
   * E: Shrimp (Ebi)

  Workstations
   * C: Cutting Board (Chopping ingredients).
   * R: Range / Stove (Cooking ingredients).
   * A: Assembly Station (Combine items here).
   * P: Plate Storage (Get clean plates here).
   * W: Washing Station (Wash dirty plates here).
   * S: Serving Counter (Deliver finished dishes here).
   * T: Trash (Throw away mistakes).

  ---

  3. How to Prepare Ingredients
  Different ingredients require different steps. If you try to use an ingredient in the wrong state (e.g., Raw Rice on a plate), the game won't let you.

  ğŸš Rice (K)
   1. Go to K, press E to pick up Raw Rice.
   2. Go to R (Stove), press E to put it in the pot.
   3. WAIT 12 seconds. The bar will turn red when done.
       * Warning: If you leave it longer than 24s, it burns!
   4. Press E to pick up the Cooked Rice.

  ğŸ¥’ Cucumber (U)
   1. Go to U, press E to pick up Raw Cucumber.
   2. Go to C (Cutting Board), press E to place it.
   3. Press E again (or wait, depending on logic) to chop. It takes 3 seconds.
   4. Press E to pick up Chopped Cucumber.

  ğŸŸ Fish (F)
   1. Go to F, press E to pick up Raw Fish.
   2. DO NOT CHOP OR COOK.
   3. It is ready to serve immediately as Raw Fish.

  ğŸ¦ Shrimp (E)
  This is the hardest one.
   1. Go to E, press E to pick up Raw Shrimp.
   2. Go to C (Cutting Board), press E to chop it (Wait 3s) -> Pick up Chopped Shrimp.
   3. Go to R (Stove), press E to cook the Chopped Shrimp.
   4. Wait 12s -> Pick up Cooked Shrimp.

  ğŸ™ Nori (N)
   1. Go to N, press E to pick up Nori.
   2. Ready immediately.

  5. Gameplay Walkthrough

  Step 1: Get a Plate
   * Go to P (Plate Storage) and pick up a clean white plate.
   * Tip: You can place the plate on a counter (like A) to free your hands while you fetch ingredients.

  Step 2: Read the Order
   * Check the top left corner. Example: "Kappa Maki".

  Step 3: Gather & Prep
   * Based on the recipe, run to the ingredient stations.
   * Cook Rice at R.
   * Chop Veggies at C.
   * Bring them to where you put your plate (Station A).

  Step 4: Assemble
   * Hold an ingredient (e.g., Cooked Rice) and press E on the Plate. The ingredient will move onto the plate.
   * Repeat until all required items are on the plate.

  Step 5: Serve
   * Pick up the finished Dish.
   * Run to S (Serving Counter).
   * Press E.
   * If correct: Score goes up!
   * If wrong: Score goes down, and you get a penalty.

  Step 6: Wash Dishes
   * After a while, dirty plates return to P.
   * Take dirty plates to W (Washing Station).
   * Press E to wash (takes 3s).
   * Take the clean plate back to P or use it immediately.
